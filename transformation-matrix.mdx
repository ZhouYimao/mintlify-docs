### Success Formulas by Role

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Founders">
    **The Formula**: Time Freedom ‚Üí Strategic Focus ‚Üí Faster Growth
    
    1. Week 1: Automate biggest time drain
    2. Week 2-3: Reinvest time in product/sales
    3. Week 4+: Scale what works
    
    **Common Mistake**: Trying to automate everything at once
    **Success Secret**: One agent, one problem, massive focus
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Solopreneurs">
    **The Formula**: Overwhelm Relief ‚Üí Capacity Unlock ‚Üí Income Multiplication
    
    1. Week 1: Automate admin tasks
    2. Week 2-3: Focus on high-value work
    3. Week 4+: Take on more clients/projects
    
    **Common Mistake**: Not trusting agents with client work
    **Success Secret**: Start with internal tasks, expand to client-facing
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Small Business">
    **The Formula**: Team Leverage ‚Üí Process Excellence ‚Üí Competitive Advantage
    
    1. Week 1: Augment overwhelmed departments
    2. Week 2-3: Standardize and scale processes
    3. Week 4+: Compete with bigger players
    
    **Common Mistake**: Not involving team in agent design
    **Success Secret**: Make team co-creators, not victims
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Enterprise">
    **The Formula**: Pilot Success ‚Üí Department Scale ‚Üí Organization Transform
    
    1. Week 1: Prove value in one department
    2. Week 2-4: Document and replicate
    3. Month 2+: Roll out systematically
    
    **Common Mistake**: Over-planning, under-executing
    **Success Secret**: Start small, show ROI, expand fast
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
